Studying

How I Stay Productive

Staying focused and organized helps me keep up with classes and assignments. Here's what helps me get into the zone:

My Study Essentials:

  • Noise-canceling headphones
  • Iced coffee or matcha
  • Planner or digital calendar
  • Group study sessions

Study Habits That Work for Me:

Pomodoro Method
Study 25 minutes, break 5. It keeps my brain fresh!
Highlight & Annotate
I highlight key terms and take margin notes in readings.
Use Campus Spaces
I love switching up my environment — libraries, cafés, lounges.
