How I Get Ready for Game Days

Whether it's football or basketball season, game days are a whole event. Here's what I do to get in the spirit:

My Gameday Checklist:

  • Pick out school colors or merch
  • Meet up with friends
  • Take photos and make snacks
  • Cheer loud and have fun!

My Favorite Game Day Things:

Tailgate Food
Chips, dips, grilled stuff, and soda or lemonade!
Outfit
Usually a team hoodie or jersey and fun accessories.
Tradition
We always take a group photo before kickoff!
